About Me

Hi there! I'm David, a full-stack developer with a passion for coding and continuous learning. I was born in Michigan, and after spending some time in Illinois as a child, I returned to Michigan, where I've spent most of my life.

Education

I attended Rockford High School and later pursued my interest in computers and technology at Grand Rapids Community College and Ferris State University. My studies laid the foundation for my career as a developer, helping me build a deep understanding of programming languages and software development methodologies.

Professional Experience

Currently, I work at Harris Computer, where I contribute to the development of Aumentum, a property tax application. My primary focus is on assessment calculations — tracking values and events related to people's homes. For a more detailed look at my experience, check out my résumé.

Hobbies & Interests

As a programmer, I love staying updated on the latest trends in technology and constantly expanding my knowledge. Outside of work, I maintain my Spanish language skills, keep my math sharp, enjoy learning piano, and make a point to exercise regularly.
